The Life of Avicii

Avicii (Tim Bergling)

Here's a time line of Tim Bergling's life:

  • 1989 - He was born in Stockholm on 8th September
  • 2005 - Began making music at the age of 16
  • 2010 -Bergling released the hit song "Seek Bromance", which reached the top 20 in several countries
  • 2011 -Bergling released "Levels", which launched him into the mainstream.
  • 2012 - His collaboration track "Sunshine" with David Guetta was nominated for a Grammy award under the category for Best Dance Recording.
  • 2013 - Bergling was nominated for a Grammy for Best Dance Recording with "Levels", he also released his fourth single off the album, "Addicted to You"
  • 2018 - Bergling passed away on 20 April near Muscat at the age of 28

"One day you'll leave this world behind. So live a life you will remember" ~Avicii
